Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

Find answers to common questions about SereniTrain and how it benefits you.

How does SereniTrain detect if I'm eligible for a refund?

Our system actively monitors train journeys and checks if they meet refund criteria set by train companies, such as delays of 30 minutes or more. If conditions are met, we automatically initiate the refund process.

How quickly will I receive a refund if my trip qualifies?

Once eligibility is confirmed, refunds are processed instantly and sent directly to your payment method. You will also receive a notification confirming the transaction.

What information can I see about my journey and refunds?

Through our platform, you can view the current status of your journey and any updates on potential refunds. If everything is on schedule, we’ll confirm it. If a refund is issued, we’ll keep you informed every step of the way.

Does SereniTrain work with all train companies?

We partner with a growing network of train operators to expand our services and enhance our coverage. Check our list of partnered companies to confirm if your provider is included.

Is there a cost for using SereniTrain's refund service?

SereniTrain is free to use for travelers. We work directly with train companies to bring you seamless service without extra fees.
